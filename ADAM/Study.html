Certification Skill Level

The Industrial Managed Switch Certification Program are divided into three different levels for participants from all genres to choose the most suitable one depending on different situations.

Level Description
iConnectivity Product Basic
(IPB)		 This level focuses on the product basic knowledge and an overall network concept for each function and protocol of EKI switches. Participants will equip the basic network knowledge and how our product plays a role in network applications; mainly aimed at the salesperson or new FAE who don’t have much related experience. Participants should know the object mentioned in the training slides and have the basic concept about the capability of the products and how it can be applied.
iConnectivity Certified Industrial Associate (ICIA) Focusing on the EKI-7700 Layer 2 Managed Switch Series and its technical details. Understand the Layer 2 switch protocols and how it’s designed to work on the product; then have the ability to dig out the possible root cause of the problem in an application system; mainly aimed at the channel partner or junior FAE. Participants should fully understand the training slides and make sure they know how to apply every function correctly.
iConnectivity Certified Industrial Profession (ICIP) Step up equipping the product knowledge of Layer 3 Managed Switch Series (EKI-9600/9700) also Layer 3 routing protocol such as OSPF and RIP. As an enhancement of ICIA Level, being familiar with the Layer 2 switch protocol mechanism details and software designs of the product; also having the ability to dig out the possible root cause of the problem in an application system. In addtion, understand the basic knowledge of L3 routing concept and the applications ; mainly aimed at the channel partner or senior FAE. Participants should not only focus on the training slides, but also the hands-on and troubleshooting skills to solve real world scenarios.

Self-Study Materials & Resources

The modularized training material of EKI-7700 Managed Switch Series covers most of the essential knowledge on both iConnectivity Product Basic (IPB) and iConnectivity Certified Industrial Associate (ICIA) skill levels of the certification. For the skill level of iConnectivity Certified Industrial Profession (ICIP), it’s extended with EKI-9600/9700 L3 Managed Switch Series related L3 knowledge and topics. Also its exam topics and hands-on part will include advanced implementation and detailed domain-know-how.

People who want to participant the certification program please REGISTER first, and the related study materials will be attached with our reply. Looking forward to your participation!
