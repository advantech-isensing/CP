Certification Skill Level

The ADAM Remote I/O series certification program are divided into three levels for participants from all genres to choose the most suitable one depending on different situations.

Level Description
iSensing Product Basic
(IPB)		 Know the function of each product and its specification, mainly aimed at the salesperson or new FAE who don’t have much related experience. Participants should know the object mentioned in the training slides and have the basic concept about how to use the ADAM modules. This level focuses on the product basic knowledge and an overall concept for each function of ADAM modules.
iSensing Certified Industrial Associate (ICIA) Understand the functions of the product and have the ability to dig out the possible root cause of the problem in an application system, mainly aimed at the channel partner or junior FAE. Participants should fully understand the training slides and make sure they know how to use these modules correctly. This level mainly focuses on the product technical details and some application scenario introduction.
iSensing Certified Industrial Profession (ICIP) Familiar with the hardware and software designs of the product and have the ability to dig out the possible root cause of the problem in an application system, mainly aimed at the channel partner or senior FAE. Participants should not only focus on the training slides, but also study the selected FAQ list. Please note that the FAQ list will be covered in the test. This level focuses on the application of the product and the hands-on about how to use the modules.

Self-Study Materials & Resources

The modularized training material of ADAM Remote I/O Series covers most of the essential knowledge on both iSensing Product Basic (IPB) and iSensing Certified Industrial Associate (ICIA) skill levels of the certification. For the skill level of iSensing Certified Industrial Profession (ICIP), exam topics may include advanced implementation and domain-know-how which covered by FAQs.

People who want to participant the certification program please REGISTER first, and the related study materials will be attached with our reply. Looking forward to your participation!
